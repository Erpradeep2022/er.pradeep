THE MISSION

"Securing the Industrial Internet of Things through Silicon-level Trust."

I am an Electronics & Telecommunication Engineer turned VLSI Researcher. My work focuses on the intersection of Cryptography and Hardware Architecture. Currently, I am a Ph.D. Scholar at NIT Rourkela, contributing to the MeitY-sponsored Chips-to-Startup (C2S) program.

My expertise spans the entire digital IC design flow, from RTL coding in Verilog to physical implementation at the 180nm node.
