Professional Profile

Reliable and resourceful VLSI Design Engineer with extensive experience in integrating cryptographic algorithms and security protocols into VLSI systems[cite: 7, 8]. Ongoing Research Frontiers

Data & Image Security

Developing robust hardware-level security mechanisms for sensitive data and high-resolution imaging systems used in critical infrastructure.

Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)

Exploring and designing ASIC architectures for lattice-based and other PQC algorithms to future-proof hardware against quantum computing threats.

ASIC Design of RISC-V

Working on specialized RISC-V processor designs that incorporate custom cryptographic instructions and secure boot mechanisms.

Lightweight Cryptography

Focusing on the ASIC implementation of NIST lightweight winners like ASCON to secure resource-constrained edge devices[cite: 14, 27, 31].
